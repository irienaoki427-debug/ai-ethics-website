Ethical Issues in AI

AI brings progress—but also challenges that society must address responsibly.

🔐 Privacy Concerns

AI systems often require massive amounts of data, raising privacy issues around surveillance, consent, and misuse of personal data.

⚖️ Bias & Fairness

AI learns from existing data. If data is biased, AI decisions may reinforce discrimination in jobs, loans, or justice systems.

🤖 Job Automation

AI is replacing repetitive tasks, but it may also displace millions of workers. Reskilling is necessary to adapt to the AI-driven job market.

💣 Autonomous Weapons

Military use of AI raises ethical debates about accountability, decision-making, and risks of lethal autonomous weapons.

AI ethics is about balance: innovation should not come at the cost of human values and rights.
